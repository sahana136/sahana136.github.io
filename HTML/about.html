Students' Council NITK

The Students’ Council of NITK consists of the elected body of students from different branches and batches. It works in the leadership of a President, a Vice-President, a Secretary and a Joint Secretary. The duration of the Council is one full academic year. The Council is also supported by committees such as Incident Committee, Engineer Committee, which coordinate major students’ festivals of the Institute.

Structure

The Students’ Council of NITK Surathkal consists of elected representatives of all departments and classes and all the other nominated persons. The duration of the Council is one full academic year. The Council is also supported by committees such as Incident Committee, Engineer Committee, which coordinate major students’ festivals of the Institute.


The Council works under the leadership of the Students’ Council President, Vice President, General Secretary and Joint Secretary.
